PANTHER PROGRAMS

Java and Swift App Development at Northshore High School

About Panther Programs

Panther Programs is a new programming club at Northshore High School in Slidell, Louisiana. Starting in August 2016, the club will prepare anyone interested in learning how to make applications for iOS and/or Android. It will teach the fundamentals of two programming languages, Java and Swift. Meetings will be every Thursday at 7.00am in Ms. Karcher's room.

Our Goal

By the end of the year, we plan to become efficient programmers and be able to develop several apps and publish them onto the App Store and Google Play Store.
